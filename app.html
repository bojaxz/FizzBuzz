Fizz Buzz App

The classic inteview question. A program that iterates from 1 to 100 replacing multiples of two given numbers with the words "Fizz" or "Buzz" and replacing multiples of both numbers with "FizzBuzz". This version takes it one step further by letting you choose your own Fizz and Buzz numbers which are then displayed in a table.

Instructions

Give it a shot by entering your numbers into the form inputs and pressing the FizzBuzz button! Watch the magic of FizzBuzz as your numbers are replaced with FizzBuzz!
